## What gets handed off when

This file is the prep. Once interviews start happening, transcripts go in `raw/` and per-interview / batch synthesis goes in `synthesis.md`.

Outreach automation (via Claude Cowork + Gmail/Calendar MCP) is **out of scope for V0**. This is intentional. When V0 retrospectives surface that outreach volume is a bottleneck, that's the signal to add the automation module.
